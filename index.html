Environment Games




Made By Mursal And Ayam




Rules


This Game Has Four Mini Games in it. It has 4 bars which are SCORE which increase every time you win a point. LOSS! , which increases every time you lose a point. Wins, which increases if you have 30 SCORE and Losses which increase after 30 LOSS!
1: Save Water- In this you need to catch raindrops which give you points and if you miss you lose a point. RIGHT and LEFT arrow keys for control.
2: Absorb Pollution- You need to click on a pollution cloud and drag it to the pollution absorber and then press SPACE to absorb it.
3: Shoot the Plastic Bottle Robot Tank- You need to click on space to shoot your spike or you can hold SPACE so that the spike gets stuck to the wall and damages the robot. UP and DOWN arrow keys for control.
4; Don't Cut Thee Trees- You are an axe and need to get through the forest without touching the trees to avoid cutting them. Your goal is to reach the arrow. UP, DOWN, RIGHT and LEFT arrow keys for control.
TIP: CLICK ON THE GREEN BUTTON TO CHANGE THE GAME.
